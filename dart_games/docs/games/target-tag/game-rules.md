# Target Tag - Game Rules

## Objective
Be the last player (or team) standing by building shields, becoming Tagged In, and eliminating your opponents!

Target Tag is a strategic survival game where players build defensive shields by hitting their assigned target number, then go on the offensive to attack opponents once Tagged In. Master shield management, strategic attacks, and the optional Hero Bonus to outlast your opponents and claim victory.

## Setup
- **Players:** 2-10 players (solo mode) or 2-5 teams of 2 players each (team mode)
- **Starting Conditions:** All players start at 0 shields, no one Tagged In
- **Configuration Options:**
  - **Shield Max:** 1-10 shields required to become Tagged In (default: 5)
  - **Hero Bonus:** Optional power-up mechanism (on/off toggle)
  - **Game Mode:** Solo (every player for themselves) or Team (2-player teams)

## How to Play

### Turn Structure
1. **Turn Start** - Current player's name is announced
2. **Throw Darts** - Player throws up to 3 darts on the dartboard
3. **Process Hits** - Each dart either builds shields or attacks opponents
4. **Status Updates** - Announcements for Tagged In, eliminations, low shields
5. **Turn End** - After 3 darts (or manual advance), turn passes to next player
6. **Repeat** - Continue until only one player/team remains

### Scoring/Progress

**Building Shields:**
- Hit your assigned **Target Number** (1-20) to gain +1 shield
- Shields accumulate until you reach Shield Max
- Example: Target = 15, Shield Max = 5
  - Hit S15 → 1 shield
  - Hit D15 → 2 shields (double counts as 2 hits)
  - Hit T15 → 3 shields (triple counts as 3 hits)

**Becoming Tagged In:**
- When shields = Shield Max, you become **Tagged In** (attack mode)
- Tagged In status displayed with pink border and badge
- Can now attack opponents by hitting THEIR target numbers
- Must maintain Tagged In status to attack

**Attacking Opponents:**
- While Tagged In, hit opponent target numbers to reduce their shields
- Each hit removes 1 shield from opponent
- If opponent has 0 shields (vulnerable), hitting their target **eliminates** them
- Eliminated players are marked with red strikethrough and 50% opacity

**Losing Tagged In Status:**
- If your shields drop below Shield Max, you lose Tagged In status
- Happens when opponents attack you while Tagged In
- Must rebuild shields to regain Tagged In status

### Special Mechanics

#### Hero Bonus (Optional)
When enabled, each player receives a secret **Hero Bonus** target:
- One number (1-20) different from any player's target number
- Required multiplier: Double OR Triple (randomly assigned)
- Hitting your Hero Bonus (e.g., D7 or T12) grants +1 shield
- Works like hitting your target number, but only for specific multiplier
- Example: Hero Bonus = D7
  - Hit D7 → +1 shield ✓
  - Hit S7 → No bonus ✗
  - Hit T7 → No bonus ✗

**Team Mode Hero Bonus:**
- All team members share the same Hero Bonus
- Any team member can hit it to benefit the team

#### Skip Turn
- Press "Skip Turn" button to skip remaining darts
- Visual markers (―) fill remaining dart slots
- Turn immediately advances to next player
- Useful for strategic play when further throws are risky

#### Edit Score
- Press "Edit Score" button during turn review
- Adjust all 3 dart scores using ring/number picker
- Recalculates shields, Tagged In status, eliminations
- Allows correction of misrecorded throws

#### Darts Removed / Remove Darts
- After throwing 3 darts or skipping turn, remove darts from board
- Press "Remove Darts" button to confirm
- Turn advances to next player
- In emulator mode, virtual darts are cleared

## Game Modes

### Solo Mode (Free-For-All)
**Every player competes individually:**
- 2-10 players
- Each player has unique target number
- Each player has unique Hero Bonus (if enabled)
- Win condition: Last player standing (not eliminated)

**Strategy Tips:**
- Build shields quickly to become Tagged In first
- Target the strongest opponents first
- Protect yourself by maintaining high shields
- Time your attacks when opponents are vulnerable (0 shields)

### Team Mode
**2-5 teams of 2 players each:**
- Turn order alternates between teams (Team 1 Player A → Team 2 Player A → Team 1 Player B → Team 2 Player B)
- All team members share the same target number
- All team members share the same team shields
- Team becomes Tagged In when shields = Shield Max
- Eliminate an entire team by reducing all opponents to 0 shields
- Team visual identity with unique icon (randomly assigned from 10 options)

**Team Coordination:**
- Teammates can both build shields for the team
- Coordinate attacks on same opponent
- One teammate can defend while other attacks
- Team shields displayed as combined total

**Win Condition: Last team standing (all other teams eliminated)**

**Strategy Tips:**
- Coordinate with your teammate
- Build shields together for faster Tagged In
- Focus fire on one opponent team
- Protect your Tagged In status as a team

## Win Conditions

### Solo Mode
- **Last player standing wins**
- Game ends when only 1 non-eliminated player remains
- Eliminated players cannot win

### Team Mode
- **Last team standing wins**
- Game ends when only 1 team has non-eliminated players
- Both team members share the victory

### Sudden Death (Future Feature)
- If time limit reached, highest shield count wins
- Tiebreaker: Total darts thrown (fewer is better)

## Edge Cases and Special Rules

- **Simultaneous Elimination:** Not possible - eliminations are sequential by dart throw order
- **Tagged In at 0 Shields:** Cannot happen - must have Shield Max shields to be Tagged In
- **Hero Bonus at Max Shields:** Hitting Hero Bonus when already at max grants no additional benefit (shields capped at max)
- **Self-Target Hitting:** Hitting your own target number while Tagged In still builds shields (strategic defense)
- **Team Member Elimination:** In team mode, if one teammate is eliminated, the other continues playing with team shields intact
- **Edit Score After Elimination:** Editing scores can revive eliminated players if changes undo eliminating hit
- **Multiple Opponents Hit in One Turn:** Each dart processes independently, so one turn can eliminate multiple opponents
- **Target Number Validation:** Menu enforces unique target numbers per player/team
- **Shield Max Range:** Enforced between 1-10 in menu screen

## Strategy Tips

**Early Game (Building Shields):**
- Focus on hitting your target number consistently
- Use Hero Bonus if you have a clear shot
- Aim for doubles/triples of your target for faster shield building
- Don't skip turns - every dart counts early

**Mid Game (Becoming Tagged In):**
- Reach Shield Max as quickly as possible
- Watch opponents' shield counts
- Position yourself to attack once Tagged In
- Maintain defensive awareness

**Late Game (Tagged In & Attacking):**
- Prioritize opponents with 0 shields (vulnerable)
- Target the strongest opponent first (most shields)
- Balance offense and defense (rebuild shields if losing Tagged In)
- Use Hero Bonus for defensive shield recovery

**Team Mode Specific:**
- Communicate with teammate (if playing in person)
- Alternate roles: one attacks, one defends
- Coordinate focus fire on single opponent team
- Build shields together to reach Tagged In faster

**Hero Bonus Strategy:**
- Know your Hero Bonus number and multiplier
- Take Hero Bonus shots when convenient, not at the expense of main strategy
- Use Hero Bonus as emergency shield recovery when losing Tagged In
- In team mode, coordinate who takes Hero Bonus shots

**Risk Management:**
- Don't overcommit to attacks if it exposes you
- Watch for opponents approaching Tagged In
- Eliminate vulnerable opponents quickly before they rebuild
- Save skip turn for strategic moments (e.g., already eliminated all opponents' shields)
